Gravyam Speciality Chemical

Gravyam is a specialty chemical manufacturing company committed to delivering advanced chemical solutions that power modern industries. The name "Grav", inspired by gravity, represents strength, stability, and reliability—the core forces that hold systems together. "Vyam", influenced by Sanskrit roots, signifies expansion, knowledge, and continuous progress. Together, Gravyam reflects a philosophy of strong scientific foundations combined with forward-looking innovation.

We specialize in the manufacture of high-performance chemical formulations, including agro emulsifiers and formulations, phosphate esters, sulfosuccinates, construction chemicals, home care surfactants, and industrial cleaning solutions. Our products are designed to improve formulation stability, enhance efficiency, and support demanding industrial applications.

At the heart of Gravyam is a strong research and development culture, led by a team of highly experienced scientists and formulation experts who continuously work on developing innovative chemistries and optimizing product performance. Our manufacturing processes follow strict quality standards, ensuring consistency, reliability, and technical excellence.

With a vision to serve both Indian and global markets, Gravyam aims to become a trusted partner for industries seeking innovative, high-quality, and performance- driven chemical solutions.

Gravyam stands for strength in science, expansion in innovation, and commitment to excellence.
