Emulsifiable in Water (EW)

Search

ECs remain one of the most widely used formulations for crop protection products worldwide.When EC formulations are mixed with water in the spray tank, they naturally create an emulsion, with tiny droplets ranging from 0.1 to 1.0μm in size. This spontaneous emulsion is achieved by carefully selecting surfactants based on their ability to emulsify the solvent system, including the active ingredient, into water.

It's the balance between water-soluble and oil-soluble surfactant components at the water/solvent interface that creates a physically stable emulsion. When applied as a spray, this diluted emulsion ensures a uniform and precise distribution of the active ingredient on the crop, which is crucial for effective pest control.
