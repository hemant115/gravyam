Jigyasu Patidar

The organization was established with a clear focus on building a business defined by precision, reliability, and long-term value in the chemical industry. Backed by a foundation in Mechanical Engineering and an MBA in Operations Management & International Business, the approach remains structured, process-driven, and globally oriented.
Emphasis is placed on disciplined execution, consistent quality, and a deep understanding of application-specific requirements across industries. Sustainable growth is driven by the integration of technical insight, operational excellence, and a commitment to continuous improvement.
The vision is to position the company as a dependable partner—delivering performance, consistency, and trust across both domestic and international markets.

Mr. Kaushal N

Mr. Kaushal N holds a Master of Science (M.Sc.) in Chemistry and serves as the Head of Research & Development, leading innovation, technology development, and product commercialization initiatives within the organization. With over 35 years of extensive experience in chemical research, surfactant chemistry, and specialty chemical formulation, he brings strong technical expertise and industry leadership.
He has worked with leading multinational organizations in the surfactants and specialty chemicals sector, contributing to the development of high-performance solutions across agro emulsifiers, adjuvants, home care and industrial cleaning chemicals, construction chemicals, polymers, paints, and pigments etc.
Mr. Kaushal possesses significant experience in new product synthesis and process scale-up, successfully transferring technologies from laboratory research to pilot plant validation and further to commercial-scale manufacturing. He has also contributed to the development of biodegradable surfactants, including sugar-based and fatty alcohol- based systems, supporting sustainable and environmentally responsible chemical innovations.

Dr. Shankar Lal Patidar,

Our organization is deeply inspired by the life and work of Dr. Shankar Lal Patidar, who dedicated over 55 years of hard work and service to the medical field as an orthopedic surgeon and social worker. His career reflects a rare combination of professional excellence, compassion, and commitment to improving lives.
He served as a Senior Orthopedic Surgeon at Red Cross Hospital for nine years, where he was actively involved in physiotherapy, occupational therapy, and rehabilitation programs. He also mentored junior doctors, nurses, and paramedical staff, contributing significantly to medical education.
Dr. Shankar Lal Patidar later became the Director of Orthopedic and Plastic Hand Surgery at a renowned hospital in Bhopal, where he established specialized Hand Surgery and Microvascular Surgery units. He completed his M.S. in Orthopedics from Gandhi Medical College, Bhopal in 1974 and further enhanced his expertise through advanced medical training and study programs in the United Kingdom, United States, and Canada.
His social work, particularly in the treatment and rehabilitation of polio patients, earned him recognition from Mother Teresa. His dedication, discipline, and lifelong commitment to healthcare continue to inspire our vision and values.
